Software Developer

Economist and Data Analyst that became a Software Developer, but how?

My journey into software development began through my work as an economist and data analyst. While analyzing trends and building models, I realized the limitations of manual processes and discovered the power of code. What started as simple automation in Python soon became a passion for building scalable software solutions. My background in economics gave me a unique perspective—approaching coding with analytical thinking, efficiency, and optimization in mind. As I deepened my knowledge, I moved from writing scripts to developing full applications, integrating data-driven decision-making with software development. Now, as a software developer, I leverage my expertise in data analysis to create intelligent, efficient, and impactful applications. My transition wasn’t a career switch—it was an evolution, embracing technology to solve problems in new and innovative ways.
